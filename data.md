# Karnavati University

## About Karnavati University

As part of the Karnavati University community, we are proud to share our story. Established in 2017 in Uvarsad, near Gandhinagar, Gujarat, we embarked on a mission to offer high-quality education across various disciplines. Our university was founded under The Gujarat Private Universities (Amendment) Act, 2017, by the Karnavati Medical and Educational Trust (KMET), non-profit organization marking the beginning of our journey towards academic excellence and innovation.

We are recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and are members of the Association of Commonwealth Universities (ACU), underscoring our commitment to maintaining high educational standards and fostering a global perspective. Our offerings span undergraduate and postgraduate programmes in fields such as management, design, law, liberal arts, commerce, media studies, and dentistry, facilitated through our six constituent colleges. These programmes are meticulously designed to equip our students with the necessary skills and knowledge to thrive in their careers.

Innovation in education is at the heart of our ethos. We leverage state-of-the-art academic infrastructure and foster collaborative partnerships with top-class universities and institutions worldwide. Our curriculum is enriched with opportunities for externships, guest lectures, certification courses, and industry visits, ensuring a comprehensive and experiential learning experience for our students.

Accessibility to quality education for all students is a cornerstone of our philosophy. We offer scholarships to deserving candidates, aiming to support a diverse range of backgrounds and economic statuses. Our campus is equipped with modern facilities, including libraries, cafeterias, classrooms, and labs, all designed to support and enhance the student learning experience.

We place a strong emphasis on career readiness and employability, with our dedicated global placement team attracting over 200 companies annually to offer a plethora of employment opportunities to our students across various sectors.

At Karnavati University, we are united in our mission to foster an environment of learning, innovation, and success, preparing our students to be future leaders and changemakers in their respective fields.

## Our Insignia

The peacock in Karnavati University's insignia symbolizes our nation's pride, embodying nobility, beauty, renewal, and immortality. This majestic bird, revered in Indian culture for wisdom, life's beauty, and integrity, reflects our commitment to excellence and knowledge diversity. Through education, we embark on a journey to achievement, embracing our heritage while nurturing growth and innovation within our community. The motto "Shikshanat Siddhi" eloquently captures the essence of Karnavati University's philosophy: the transformative journey from education to achievement. This punchline reflects our core belief that through diligent learning and personal growth, one can attain success and fulfillment. It underscores the university's commitment to fostering an environment where education serves as the cornerstone for achieving greatness in life and career.

# Unitedworld Institute of Technology (UIT)

## About UIT

Unitedworld Institute of Technology (UIT) is dedicated to developing the next generation of leaders in engineering, science, and technology. With a curriculum designed to establish a strong base in engineering fundamentals, students are encouraged to work across disciplines, applying their skills to solve practical problems. UIT fosters a student-centric learning environment that promotes active learning and application. Engineering education here focuses on understanding the mechanics behind how things function and finding viable solutions using scientific methods and theory application. Preparing students for the comprehensive role of engineers, the programme covers designing, evaluating, developing, testing, modifying, installing, inspecting, and maintaining a vast range of products and systems, ensuring graduates are well-equipped for their engineering careers.

## Why UIT?

1. **Scholarship Opportunities**: Government Scholarships for B.tech students and Defence Scholarship for Expired Service, Injured/Retired, Serving Personnel.
2. **Internship and Placement Support**: 100+ company linkages, 100% placement assistance.
3. **State-of-the-art Infrastructure**: Modern Labs, Workshops, Libraries, Hostels, Classrooms, and other facilities.
4. **Innovation and Incubation**: On campus Karnavati University Innovation and Incubation Centre (KUIC) supports students in developing their ideas and turning them into successful ventures.
5. **Google Certification**: Industry recognized Certification to enhance employability.
6. **Globally recognized and UGC approved degrees**.

## Admission Process

1. **General Category Applicants**: A minimum 65% aggregate score in the last qualifying examination i.e. 10+2 pass/appearing with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics or Equivalent to 10 +2 from any recognized higher secondary boards as per the list of Council of Boards of School Education (COBSE) List.

2. **SC/ST/OBC Category Applicants**: A minimum 50% aggregate score in the last qualifying examination i.e. 10+2 pass/appearing with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics. Equivalent to 10 +2 from any recognized higher secondary boards as per the list of COBSE List.

3. **Selection**: Best on JEE MAINS 2025/GUJCET2025 Followed by Personal Interview.

### B.Tech Computer Science and Engineering

#### Overview

The B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering is a comprehensive programme designed to equip students with foundational knowledge and cutting-edge skills in computing, programming, and information technology. This course delves into software development, system design, machine learning, and artificial intelligence, preparing students for innovative solutions in the tech industry. Through a blend of theoretical concepts and practical projects, students gain the expertise needed to tackle complex engineering challenges and adapt to rapid technological advancements. The curriculum fosters analytical thinking, problem-solving abilities, and creativity, ensuring graduates are ready to contribute to the evolving landscape of computer science and engineering.

#### Programme Educational Objectives

1. To prepare graduates with a strong foundation in Computer Science and Applications and problem solving & programming skills in order to build successful careers professionals in industry, government, academia, research, entrepreneurial pursuit and consulting firms.
2. To equip students with analytical, design, development and soft skill to find innovative solutions to the real-world problems in collaboration with industry and professional societies.
3. To inculcate entrepreneurship, managerial skills and team work in our students through demonstration of good analytical, design and implementation skills for the betterment of individuals and society at large.
4. To produce graduates who are ethical, socially responsible and lifelong learners to fulfill their goals.

#### Programme Outcomes

1. **Engineering Knowledge**: Apply the knowledge of mathematics, science, engineering fundamentals and engineering specialization to the solution of complex engineering problems.
2. **Problem Analysis**: Identify, formulate, research literature and analyze complex engineering problems reaching substantiated conclusions using first principles of mathematics, natural sciences and engineering sciences.
3. **Design/Development of Solutions**: Design solutions for complex engineering problems and design systems, components or processes that meet specified needs with appropriate consideration for public health and safety, and cultural, societal, and environmental considerations.
4. **Investigation**: Conduct investigations into complex problems with consideration of research-based knowledge and research methods including design of experiments, analysis and interpretation of data, and synthesis of information to provide valid conclusions.
5. **Modern Tool Usage**: Create, select and apply appropriate techniques, resources and modern engineering and IT tools including prediction and modeling to complex engineering activities with an understanding of the limitations.
6. **The Engineer and Society**: Apply engineering knowledge and reasoning to assess societal, health, safety, legal and cultural issues and the responsibilities inherent in engineering practice.
7. **Environment and Sustainability**: Understand the impact of professional engineering work in societal and environmental contexts and demonstrate the knowledge of and need for sustainable development.
8. **Ethics**: Apply ethical principles and commit to professional ethics and responsibilities and norms of engineering practice.
9. **Individual and Team Work**: Function effectively as an individual, and as a member or leader in diverse teams and in multidisciplinary settings.
10. **Communication**: Communicate effectively on complex engineering activities with the engineering community and with society at large, being able to comprehend and write effective reports and design documentation, make effective presentations, and give and receive clear instructions.
11. **Project Management and Finance**: Demonstrate knowledge and understanding of engineering and management principles and apply these to one's own work, as a member and leader in a team, to manage projects and in multidisciplinary environments.
12. **Lifelong Learning**: Recognize the need for and have the preparation and ability to engage in independent and lifelong learning in the context of technological change.

#### Programme Specific Outcomes

1. Ability to work with emerging technologies in computing.
2. Demonstrate Engineering Practice learned through industry internship and research project to solve live problems in various domains

#### Career Path

Graduates of a B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering have a broad spectrum of career opportunities in the technology sector. They can pursue roles in software development, system engineering, data analysis, and cybersecurity within tech companies, startups, and multinational corporations. The degree also opens doors to innovative fields like artificial intelligence, machine learning, and blockchain technology. With the tech industry's rapid growth, graduates can find themselves working on cutting-edge projects, developing new software solutions, or enhancing cybersecurity measures. Additionally, the analytical and problem-solving skills acquired during the programme prepare graduates for roles in technology consulting, project management, and technical leadership, making them highly sought after in the global job market.
